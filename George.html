SUPER LEAGUE

WHAT IS SUPER LEAGUE?

Super League is a 16 team fantasy football league with weekly payouts of $50 for high score and 5 end of season payouts.

ROSTERS


Rosters total 16 players. 8 starters and 8 bench. You can start any nine players your want. The positions are:


  • -Quarterback

  • -Running Back

  • -Wide Reciever

  • -Tight End

  • -Kicker

  • -Defensive Line

  • -Linebacker

  • -Defensive Back

Click STARTING LINEUPS and ROSTERS for examples.

SCORING


Offensive players are scored by Touchdowns, Yards, Receptions, and Turnovers.

Kickers get points for both the yardage of the Field Goal and Field Goals made.

Defenders score with Sacks, Tackles, Passes Defended, Turnovers, and Turnover Yards.

Quarterbacks are scored on a Tier System. The first TD is worth 2 points and increases by 1 for every TD. The first 100 yards are .02 per yard and increase by .01 for every 100 yards. This makes it so Quarterbacks outside of the top tier are not overpowered.


Click SCORING for detailed scoring rules.

Click TOP 300 Scoring to see how players rank within the system. You can also sort them by position.

SCHEDULE


There are two 8 team divisions. The divisions are placed by draft order. Picks 1 - 8 will be in a division together and picks 9 - 16 will be in a division together.

All 16 teams play each other in double headers the first 8 weeks. Week 9 through 14 teams will play each other head to head within the division. In total there are 22 games. Every team within a division will have played each other 2 times by the end of the season.

Week 15 starts the playoffs. The division champions will play the #2 Seeds across divisions. There will be 3 more brackets for seeds #5 through #8, #9 through #12, and #13 through #16. The top seeds will play the lower seeds across divisions in each bracket. Tie breaks will be decided by Head to Head first, All Play second, and Overall Points third.
